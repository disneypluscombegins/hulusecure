Step-by-Step Guide to Hulu.com Activate: Unlock Your Streaming Experience


To activate Hulu, visit hulu.com/activate on a computer or mobile device, sign in to your account, and enter the unique code shown on your TV screen. Once submitted, your device will refresh, granting full access to Hulu’s streaming content.

Following are the steps to enter the code to activate Hulu:

  • Open Hulu on your TV/device and note the activation code.
  • Visit hulu.com/activate on another device.
  • Sign in to your Hulu account.
  • Enter the activation code and click "Submit."
  • Wait for confirmation—your TV will update automatically.
